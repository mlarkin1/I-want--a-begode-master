What makes the wheel special

Science meets design

Each wheel has been designed to be ergonomically comfortable. The design is minimalistic yet having all the essentials for durablity.

Maximal Comfort

The Master has a built in seat for those inclided to ride seated. It also has pads that you can use to lock in and push the Master to speed heights.

Recycled Components

One of the hallmarks of the Master is the use of recycled products that make up the body. Many of the components are made from recycled metals.
